Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
By clicking on one of the four crystals below you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
How to win: match your score to the random number given.
How to lose: if your score exceeds the random number given.
The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time the game starts the value of the crystals will change.
Good luck!
